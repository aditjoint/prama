About PRAMA

PRAMA Productions is an independent creative consultant that collaborates in the domain of creative scriptwriting for the dynamic world of films and web series content. Specializing in scriptwriting, screenplay analysis, and creative consultancy, PRAMA Productions brings together artistic vision and creative output to help filmmakers, writers, and producers craft compelling narratives that resonate with audiences.
