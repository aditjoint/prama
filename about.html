About Us

ADAM Consult, an initiative of ADIT Joint LLP, serves as strategic advisors and solution partners to organizations navigating the convergence of Digital and IT systems, sustainability goals, and human-centric innovation.

PRAMA Productions is an independent creative association that collaborates with ADAM Consult in a purely IT & marketing partnership capacity. This collaboration is limited to promotional alignment only. PRAMA Productions is not a subsidiary, division, or legal/commercial extension of ADAM Consult, and no customer engagement, claim, or transaction with PRAMA Productions shall create any legal, financial, or commercial binding on ADAM Consult.

Specializing in scriptwriting, screenplay analysis, and creative consultancy, PRAMA Productions brings together artistic vision and creative output to help filmmakers, writers, and producers craft compelling narratives that resonate with audiences. Its core expertise extends to script development and lyrics writing for screen, where language and culture are celebrated as powerful tools of expression.

With a multidisciplinary team of scriptwriters, lyricists, and creative consultants, PRAMA Productions collaborates closely with clients to co-create impactful stories. From shaping fresh screenplays to refining existing scripts, from creative brainstorming to project guidance, its approach is always rooted in authenticity, cinematic appeal, and audience engagement.
