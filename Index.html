Film & Entertainment Scriptwriting / Consultancy Services

1. Feature Film / Web Series Script Writing

Screenplay development in Hindi, English, and Urdu for feature films, OTT web series, and independent projects.

  • Original stories, adaptations, and remakes
  • Outline, treatment, step-outline, and screenplay drafts
  • Shooting script with scene-level details

2. Script Analysis & Editing (Script Doctor)

Expert review and structural enhancement of your screenplay, ensuring production-ready output.

  • Coverage reports & structural feedback
  • Character arc mapping and pacing checks
  • Annotated scripts with rewrite recommendations

3. Hindi-Urdu Songs & Ghazals Writing

Professional lyric writing tailored for films, series, and independent music projects.

  • Film songs, title tracks, thematic songs
  • Classical and contemporary ghazals
  • Rhyme schemes and metre notes for composers

4. Hindi Language Correction

Refinement of scripts in Hindi for films, TV serials, and web series with linguistic accuracy.

  • Register correction (formal/informal balance)
  • Idiomatic accuracy and cultural localization
  • Subtitle-friendly phrasing for OTT releases

5. Dialogue Diction Monitoring (Studios)

On-site or remote monitoring during recording sessions to ensure dialogue authenticity.

  • Real-time pronunciation and diction checks
  • Dialectal authenticity & emotional tone monitoring
  • Session notes with corrective instructions

6. Hindi Language Monitoring (Studios)

Ensuring overall quality and correctness of all Hindi language content in recordings.

  • Grammar and pronunciation audits
  • Consistency across dubbing & voiceovers
  • Final QA reports with recommendations

7. Hindi Diction Tuition for Actors

Coaching sessions for actors to perfect diction, accent, and performance-oriented delivery.

  • One-on-one or group training
  • Accent training and breath control
  • Session recordings for practice

8. English ‚Üí Hindi Translation

Creative translations optimized for films, OTT, and general entertainment content.

  • Lip-sync friendly translations
  • Multiple register versions (formal/informal)
  • Subtitle-ready scripts with time codes
