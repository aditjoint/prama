Film & Entertainment — Scriptwriting / Consultancy Services

End-to-end creative and technical script services: feature films, web series, TV serials, songs & ghazals, dialogue diction, language correction and studio monitoring.

Core Services (Technical Overview)

We provide creative writing combined with production-ready technical deliverables. Each service includes logs, versioned drafts (PDF/Final Draft/FDX), and delivery notes.

  • 1. Feature Film / Web Series Script Writing — Hindi / English / Urdu
    Original screenplay, adaptations, and bilingual scripts. Deliverables: outline, treatment (3–12 pages), step-outline, full draft (screenplay), shooting draft, and scene-level beat sheets.
  • 2. Script Analysis / Editing — Script Doctor
    Structural analysis, character arc mapping, act-break diagnostics, pacing & tone audit, and full-line edits. Deliverables: coverage report, gap analysis, annotated script (PDF/FDX) and recommended rewrite plan.
  • 3. Hindi / Urdu Songs & Ghazals Writing
    Lyric writing for film songs, title tracks, and independent ghazals. Includes multiple lyric versions, metre/rhyme schemes, and melody-guided syllabic notes for composers.
  • 4. Hindi Language Correction — Feature Film / Web / TV
    Localization, register correction (formal/informal mix), idiom optimization, and subtitle-friendly phrasing. Deliverables: corrected scripts and change-log with justification.
  • 5. Dialogue Diction Monitoring at Recording Studios
    On-site/remote diction supervision during ADR/recording to ensure dialectal authenticity, sync accuracy and emotional intent. Real-time notations and take-sheet tagging provided.
  • 6. Hindi Language Monitoring of All Contents at Recording Studios
    Quality assurance of narration, dubbing and voiceover content for grammar, pronunciation and cultural sensitivity. Includes final QA report and corrective markups.
  • 7. Diction Tuition for Actors (Hindi)
    Tailored coaching sessions (individual / ensemble) covering diction, breath control, regional accents and performance-led delivery. Sessions can be recorded and shipped as MP4 for review.
  • 8. English → Hindi Translation (Films / Entertainment / General)
    Creative translation optimized for lip-sync and cultural relevance, with multiple register options and subtitle-friendly variants. Deliverables: translated script (time-coded) and translation notes.
Output formats: PDF, Final Draft (FDX), Fountain, DOCX, SRT
Turnaround: Outline 3–5 days · First draft 10–21 days (project dependent)
Revision cycles: Standard 2 rounds (additional by agreement)

Typical Workflow (technical)

  1. Briefing & assets intake (references, treatments, runtime targets)
  2. Concept & treatment (approved before full scripting)
  3. Step-outline & scene breakdown
  4. Drafting (Act I/II/III) with weekly milestone deliveries
  5. Script editing — table reads — language correction
  6. Final delivery: production/ shooting script + marked changes + sync materials

Technical & Production Notes

  • All scripts are versioned with semantic versioning (v1.0-draft, v1.1-rev1). We provide a change-log and line-level annotations for production teams.
  • We follow standard screenplay formatting (Courier 12pt equivalence) and can convert to industry formats on request.
  • Studio monitoring includes real-time timestamped notes (HH:MM:SS) for ADR and dubbing sessions.
  • We supply lip-sync optimization and phonetic notes for translated dialogues to assist ADR artists.
